About Us

Through the provision of reliable IT support services for a diverse clientele base across West Africa, Edger Verve Technologies has enabled firms to attain a cost-saving efficiency of about 35%. Our model is built on the provision of reliable solutions coupled with an exceptional customer service support system. Today, we are an Advanced Business Partner of International Business Machines (IBM) in Nigeria and we are working towards having a similar strategic partnership with other OEMs, such as we perceive the opportunities in the industry. Our goal is to become the major technical delivery partners to major Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) in our region by the close of the decade. Our range of services includes technical support services, solution design scoping, relocation services, managed & outsourced services and project management. We also provide solutions in the following areas: virtualization, security, disaster recovery, enterprise hardware and Cloud.

Edger Verve is an ICT solutions company that offers strategic outsourcing, IT infrastructure management services and technical support services that enable corporations efficiently optimize their operational capabilities as well as increase system uptime and minimize revenue loss. Edger Verve was founded in December 2014 with a commitment to provide custom IT services that are responsive to client needs and provide exceptional customer service support. Our business model is built on the values of diligence, excellent service delivery and customer satisfaction. With a wealth of over 15 years of hands-on experience in the deployment and support of enterprise IT solutions, our team is armed with the requisite expertise you require to attain an optimized IT system as well as tailor your business processes to save cost and ensure maximum productivity.
